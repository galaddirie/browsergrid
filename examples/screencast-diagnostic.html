Quick Fix Guide

If screencast is stuck pending:

  • Step 1: Make sure BrowserMux is running and connected
  • Step 2: Navigate your browser to a real webpage (NOT chrome:// or about:blank)
  • Step 3: Try opening a simple page like: https://example.com
  • Step 4: Check the logs above for any error messages
  • Step 5: Look at BrowserMux server logs for CDP errors
